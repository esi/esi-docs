## About

Socket.Kill is a real-time killmail platform with a distinctive visual identity — fusing EVE Online aesthetics with CRT monitor effects from the *Alien* franchise. Phosphor green on black, scanline overlays, terminal-style readouts, and atmospheric touches throughout that reward closer attention.

The platform is engineered for speed. Killmails stream in from R2Z2 and are processed, broadcast, and forwarded in real time, with sub-50ms ingest-to-broadcast latency. The feed is designed to feel *present* — a live window into New Eden rather than a record of what happened.

Shortlisted for the **[CCP Fanfest 2026 New Developer of the Year](https://www.eveonline.com/news/view/meet-your-eve-creator-awards-finalists)** award.

## Features

- **Live killmail feed** with sub-50ms ingest-to-broadcast latency
- **Discord relay** with 11 curated categories (capital losses, officer sightings, Pochven activity, whale kills, suicide ganks, and more)
- **Region heatmap** visualising kill density across New Eden in real time
- **Global NPC death counter** tracking ratting activity across new eden since 14th January 2026
- **Public REST and Whitelist based WebSocket APIs** for third-party integrators
- **System/Region Filter** Ability to filter kills based on a system of choice.
